# Ownly MVP Scope

The MVP should prove one hard promise: a purchased item can become a complete, searchable, living digital asset with almost no manual work.

## Must Ship

- Workspace-based auth and permissions.
- Upload sessions for invoices, receipts, screenshots, PDFs, and email invoice files.
- Product profile creation from confirmed extraction.
- Product profile CRUD.
- Document-to-product linking.
- Warranty and return deadline capture.
- Dashboard metrics.
- Natural language search endpoint and UI.
- OCR and AI adapters behind clear interfaces.

## Should Ship In Private Beta

- Gmail invoice detection.
- Official manual finder.
- AI chat with document citations.
- Duplicate product detection.
- Resale estimate v1.
- Family workspace invitations.

## Later

- Bank transaction matching.
- Browser extension.
- Native mobile apps.
- Offline mode.
- QR-code digital receipt partnerships.
- Repair partner and resale marketplace integrations.
